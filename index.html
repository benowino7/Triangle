Triangles

There are many different types of triangles:

  • Equilateral triangle
  • Isosceles triangle
  • Scalene triangle
  • Right-angle triangle

most people oftenly forget that:
  • An Equilateral triangle can be an Isosceles triangle but an Isosceles triangle cannot be Equilateral.
  • A Scalene triangle can be a Right-angle triangle and not every Right-angle triangle is scalene triangle
  • A right Right-angle triangle can be both Isosceles and scalene in different forms.

Examples

Equilateral triangle

This is a triangle that
all its sides are equal
together with its angles.

Isosceles triangle

This is a type of triangle that
has only two of its sides equal
not the third side.

Scalene triangle

This is a type of triangle that
none of its side is equal to the other
but its values form a triangle.

Right-angle triangle

This is a type of triangle that
has a right angle (90) and
can be either Isosceles or scalene triangle.
It is only different in that the square of its larger
side is equal to the sum of the square of the other
two remaining sides
